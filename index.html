Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us about our coding courses, including:

  • Your name and contact details (email address, phone number)

  • Information about your child's interests and experience with technology
  • Any special requirements or preferences for lessons

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide information about our coding courses
  • Schedule and conduct coding lessons

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share information with:

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

What will I need for the course?

The ideal setup is one large screen split in half - one side for the lesson, one side for coding. No large monitor? No problem - a laptop and tablet combination works perfectly too. We'll walk you through the simple setup process so your child can focus on learning.

I'm a parent, how can I track my child's progress?

Our courses are "project based" meaning that your child will develop a portfolio of accomplishments to demonstrate their progress.

What if I miss a lesson?

Lesson recordings are provided if a student was to miss a lesson.
